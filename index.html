About This Study

The Arc of Creation

These five texts tell a complete biblical story about God's purpose for the world. Using the Bible Project's visual commentaries, we journey from God's initial design through humanity's vocation, and finally to the Creator stepping into creation to rescue it.

Each week connects to our church's focus statement — Life means more — through five themes: creative, ordered, generous, loving, and resurrection.

What to Expect

Each Week's Format

Step 1
Watch the Video

We'll begin each session by watching a Bible Project visual commentary together — about 10 minutes of beautifully animated biblical scholarship.

Step 2
Discuss Together

React to the video's main ideas, then dive deeper into the biblical text with focused discussion questions for each passage.

Step 3
Prepare for Next Week

At the end of each session, you'll find links to the study notes for the following week so you can read ahead and come prepared.
